Welcome to Laurel Mountain Hollow

Laurel Mountain Hollow is a woodland farm located in the Hamlet of Spencertown, NY. Nestled in the western fringe of the Taconic Mountains in Northern Columbia County, the hollow has all slopes feeding two pure mountain brooks which empty into the wetlands at the center of the property. This unspoiled area provides the perfect conditions for the 61 acre sugarbush and apiary. Working in collaboration with nature, all of our products are hand crafted in small batches and have no added sugars or preservatives.
