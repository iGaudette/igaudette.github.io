About

Gaudette Woodworks is the solo project of Ian Gaudette and is based in Greenville, South Carolina. All projects are original designs aiming to blend both form and function. Traditional joinery methods are used to create pieces that will last a lifetime. This joinery is exposed on many of the pieces, allowing an observer both see and feel how each was constructed. Please enjoy this gallery of some recently completed projects.

Gaudette Woodworks

Dining Chair

Details: This chair was made as a set of six to match a dining room table. The shaped back, upholstered seat, and slight backwards tilt combine to make the chair comfortable even while sitting for long periods of time. The legs are attached to the rails using loose tenons which were pinned on one side and drawbored on the other. These pins were made from a lighter species of wood to provide contrast. Subtle curves and tapers provide additional interest to the piece.

Wood: Back Walnut

Finish: Osmo Polyx Hardwax-Oil 3043 Clear Satin

Dimensions: 18" deep x 18" wide x 38" tall
