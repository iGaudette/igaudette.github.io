Welcome to Laurel Mountain Hollow

Laurel Mountain Hollow is a woodland farm located in the Hamlet of Spencertown, NY. Nestled in the western fringe of the Taconic Mountains in Northern Columbia County the hollow has all slopes feeding two pure mountain brooks which empty into the wetlands at the center of the property. This unspoiled area provides the perfect conditions for the 61acre sugarbush and apiary. Working in collaboration with what Mother Nature started, all of our products are meant to bring the tranquility and sweetness of our hollow to your home.
